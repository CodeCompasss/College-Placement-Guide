# Offer Evaluation Guide

Not all high salaries are better offers — evaluate the entire compensation package.

---

## 🎯 Goal
Decide whether to accept, reject, or negotiate an offer based on total value.

---

## 📦 Key Components of an Offer

- Base Salary
- Bonus (performance/annual)
- Stocks (RSUs/ESOPs)
- Joining Bonus
- Benefits (insurance, PTO, perks)

---

## 🧠 Evaluation Framework

### 1. Base Salary Stability
- Fixed monthly income
- Important for financial safety

---

### 2. Variable Pay
- Performance bonuses
- Sales/target-based pay
- Can be uncertain

---

### 3. Equity (Stocks)
- High risk, high reward
- Check vesting schedule (usually 4 years)

---

### 4. Work-Life Balance
- Work hours
- Remote/hybrid flexibility
- Burnout risk

---

### 5. Career Growth
- Learning opportunities
- Tech stack
- Brand value

---

## 📊 Simple Scoring Method

Rate each out of 10:
- Salary stability
- Growth opportunity
- Work-life balance
- Brand value

If total ≥ 32/40 → Strong offer

---

## ❌ Red Flags
- No clarity on stock value
- Unrealistic bonus structure
- Excessive overtime culture
- No growth path

---

## 🚀 Pro Tip
A slightly lower salary at a top company often beats higher salary in low-growth roles.
