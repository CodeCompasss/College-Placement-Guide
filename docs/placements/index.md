Landing your dream job requires more than just good grades. This guide is designed to provide you with a structured path to navigate both on-campus and off-campus recruitment cycles.

## 🗝️ Core Preparation Pillars

1.  **Aptitude & Logic:** The first filter in most companies.
2.  **CS Fundamentals:** OS, DBMS, Networking, and OOPs.
3.  **Coding (DSA):** The backbone of the technical interview.
4.  **Soft Skills:** Communication, body language, and confidence.

> [!TIP]
> **Start early!** The best time to start preparing was yesterday. The second best time is today.
