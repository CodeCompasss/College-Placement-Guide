# LinkedIn Setup Guide

## 1. Profile Photo
- Clear face photo
- Neutral background
- Professional appearance

---

## 2. Banner
- Tech-related or minimal design
- Add tagline like:
  "Aspiring Software Engineer | DSA | Web Dev"

---

## 3. Headline (VERY IMPORTANT)
Bad:
"Student at XYZ College"

Good:
"Aspiring Software Engineer | Java | DSA | Full Stack Developer | Open to Internships"

---

## 4. About Section

Structure:
- Who you are
- Skills
- Projects
- Career goal

Example:
"I am a Computer Science student passionate about building scalable systems..."

---

## 5. Experience & Projects
- Add all major projects
- Link GitHub demos

---

## 6. Skills Section
- Add 20–30 relevant skills
- Prioritize technical skills

---

## 7. Activity
- Post coding progress
- Share projects
- Engage with recruiters

---

## 8. Optimization Tip
Your LinkedIn = your public resume + proof of work
