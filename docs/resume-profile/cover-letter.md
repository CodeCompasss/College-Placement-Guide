# Cover Letter Guide

## 1. Purpose
A cover letter explains:
- Why this company
- Why this role
- Why you

---

## 2. Structure

### Opening Paragraph
- Introduce yourself
- Mention role applied
- Show enthusiasm

Example:
"I am a Computer Science student passionate about backend development..."

---

### Middle Paragraph (MOST IMPORTANT)
- Highlight 1–2 key projects or skills
- Connect them to job role

Example:
"I built a scalable REST API using Node.js..."

---

### Why Company
- Mention something specific about company
- Products, culture, tech stack

---

### Closing
- Express interest in interview
- Thank them

---

## 3. Template

Dear Hiring Manager,

I am applying for the [Role] at [Company]. I am a [year/branch] student with experience in [skills].

I have worked on [project], where I [impact].

I am particularly interested in [company] because [reason].

I would love the opportunity to contribute to your team.

Sincerely,  
[Your Name]

---

## 4. Tips
✔ Keep under 200–300 words  
✔ Personalize for every company  
✔ Avoid repeating resume
