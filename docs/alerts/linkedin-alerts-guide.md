# LinkedIn Alerts Guide (High Impact System)

This file explains how to turn LinkedIn into a **job notification + recruiter attraction system**.

---

# 1. Turn ON Job Alerts

- Go to LinkedIn Jobs
- Search: "Software Engineer Intern"
- Click: “Create alert”
- Set frequency: Daily

---

# 2. Follow Companies (VERY IMPORTANT)

Follow:

- FAANG companies
- Indian product companies
- Startups hiring frequently

This triggers:
- job posts
- recruiter visibility

---

# 3. Recruiter Alert Strategy

Search and follow:

- "Technical Recruiter"
- "Talent Acquisition"
- "SDE Hiring"

Turn on notifications.

---

# 4. Post Engagement Alerts

Engage with:
- hiring posts
- recruiter posts
- internship announcements

This increases algorithm visibility.

---

# 5. Keyword Strategy

LinkedIn shows jobs based on keywords:

Use:
- "SDE"
- "Backend Developer"
- "Full Stack"
- "Software Engineer Intern"

---

# 6. Notification Settings

Enable:
- Job recommendations
- Company updates
- Messages from recruiters

---

# 7. Pro Tip

Commenting on hiring posts increases profile reach by 3–5x.
