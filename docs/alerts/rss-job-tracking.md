# 📡 RSS Job Tracking System

RSS lets you **receive job postings instantly at the moment they are published**, without manually checking websites.

Think of it as:

> A real-time job “data stream” directly into your feed.

---

# 🎯 Core Idea

Most candidates:

* search jobs manually ❌
* refresh LinkedIn daily ❌

Top candidates:

* receive jobs automatically ⚡
* apply earlier than 80–90% of applicants

---

# 🔧 1. What RSS Actually Does

RSS (Really Simple Syndication) automatically pushes updates when:

* New jobs are posted
* Company career pages update
* Remote job boards publish listings

---

# 🧠 Why This Works (Important Insight)

RSS bypasses:

* algorithm feeds
* job ranking systems
* LinkedIn visibility delays

👉 You get **raw, unfiltered job data instantly**

# ⚙️ 4. Setup System (Feedly Method)

### Step-by-step:

1. Open [Feedly](https://feedly.com?utm_source=chatgpt.com)
2. Click **“Add Content”**
3. Paste job RSS links
4. Create folders:

* Internships
* SDE Jobs
* Remote Jobs
* Startup Roles

---

# 🧠 5. Filtering Strategy (Critical)
